========================================================
USER STORIES — SUPPLIER / IMPORT PARTNER
Project: American Computers Albania — Supply Chain AI
========================================================

ROLE DESCRIPTION:
Suppliers are EU-based technology distributors who supply
products to American Computers Albania. They include
companies from Italy, Germany, Turkey, Greece, France,
Hungary and the Netherlands.

--------------------------------------------------------
STORY 1 — Supplier Profile Visibility
--------------------------------------------------------
As a Supplier,
I want my company profile to be visible in the system
So that American Computers Albania staff can see my contact
details, rating and performance metrics.

Acceptance Criteria:
- Supplier directory shows name, country, city and contact email
- Rating out of 5 is displayed for each supplier
- On-time delivery rate percentage is shown
- Average lead time in days is visible

--------------------------------------------------------
STORY 2 — On-Time Delivery Performance
--------------------------------------------------------
As a Supplier,
I want my on-time delivery rate to be tracked accurately
So that American Computers Albania can evaluate my reliability
and I can improve my service.

Acceptance Criteria:
- On-time delivery rate is calculated from actual vs estimated days
- Rate above 90% is shown in green
- Rate between 80-90% is shown in amber
- Rate below 80% is shown in red

--------------------------------------------------------
STORY 3 — Order History
--------------------------------------------------------
As a Supplier,
I want all orders placed with me to be tracked in the system
So that there is a clear record of our business relationship.

Acceptance Criteria:
- Orders table shows origin city and country for each order
- Supplier ID is linked to each order
- Order status is tracked from processing to delivered
- Total order value in EUR is recorded

--------------------------------------------------------
STORY 4 — Lead Time Comparison
--------------------------------------------------------
As a Supplier,
I want my average lead time to be compared against other suppliers
So that I can see how competitive my delivery times are.

Acceptance Criteria:
- Average lead time chart compares all suppliers side by side
- My lead time is clearly identifiable in the chart
- Lower lead time is considered better performance
